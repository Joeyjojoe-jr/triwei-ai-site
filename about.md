TriWei AI is a noncommercial source-linking and research website for people trying to understand artificial intelligence without confusing repetition, promotion, or machine-generated prose with verified knowledge.

## What TriWei currently publishes

TriWei may publish:

- direct links to original authors, publishers, repositories, companies, and public institutions;
- source metadata such as title, author, publisher, date, and source type;
- source-dated specifications or measurements reproduced with an inspectable citation;
- transparent descriptions of automated collection methods and their limitations; and
- original TriWei analysis only after human research, human authorship, source review, attribution review, and approval.

## What TriWei withholds

TriWei does not publish AI-written synopses of third-party articles that TriWei did not independently research and human-author. It also does not present:

- headline frequency as proof of importance or truth;
- automated tags as findings of wrongdoing, legality, safety, or merit;
- provider claims as independent adjudication;
- vendor specifications as independent benchmarks;
- source inclusion as endorsement; or
- a collected sample as a complete account of the field.

When a direct original source cannot be identified, TriWei prefers an incomplete folder or visible source-gap notice over an intermediary link, copied text, guessed attribution, or generated substitute.

## How to use the site

Use the rotating folders on the [homepage](/) to open original sources and navigate into narrower topic folders. Use [Signal History](/signals/), [AI Hardware](/hardware/), [Industry Atlas](/industry/), and [Ethics Watch](/ethics/) as source registers and dated data references—not as final judgments.

The [Sources & Method](/sources/) page contains the binding publication contract. The [Corrections & Revisions](/corrections/) page records material changes and explains how to report a problem.

## Limits

TriWei is curated, incomplete, and subject to error. It is not a substitute for reading the original source, consulting a qualified professional, or conducting independent research. It does not provide legal, medical, financial, safety, procurement, or investment advice.
